# Microbiome4Veterinarians

A repository for the review article **"The Microbiome for Veterinarians"**, targeting submission to *Microbiome Research Reports*.

## Contents

- **[The_microbiome_for_veterinarians.md](The_microbiome_for_veterinarians.md)** — Full review manuscript in Markdown format
- **The microbiome for clinicians.pdf** — Reference article by Porcari et al. (2025), *Cell* 188:2836–44

## Overview

This review bridges the gap between microbiome science and veterinary clinical practice. It provides clinical veterinarians with a comprehensive, evidence-based overview of:

- The microbiome as a diagnostic and therapeutic tool across veterinary species (companion animals, horses, livestock, poultry, aquaculture)
- Fecal microbiota transplantation (FMT), probiotics, and emerging microbiome therapeutics in veterinary medicine
- The **One Health** and **One Medicine** frameworks connecting human, animal, and environmental microbiomes
- Antimicrobial resistance at the human–animal–environment interface
- Actions needed to accelerate clinical translation of microbiome science in veterinary practice

All cited literature is from peer-reviewed sources and is verifiable.
